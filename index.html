Learn About HackathonAfrica

The AfricaPlan Foundation’s HackathonAfrica Bootcamp Training Program provides a dynamic 3-month immersive training experience tailored to turn college graduates into proficient Junior Web Developers. Through a comprehensive curriculum and hands-on learning approach, participants are equipped with the essential skills and knowledge necessary to excel in today’s fast-paced technology landscape.

What's Our Approach?

Empowering Individuals for Success in Today's Evolving Workforce.

1
Training

We provide comprehensive training programs tailored to equip beneficiaries with both in-demand software development skills and essential soft skills.

2
Internship

Our internship program offers valuable hands-on experience, allowing participants to apply the skills they've acquired in real-world settings.

3
Employment

Upon completion of the internship, participants transition into employment opportunities where they can further cultivate their talents and contribute meaningfully to the workforce.
